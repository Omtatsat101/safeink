How it works

Four steps. Zero identity exposure.

Write your letter

No login, no email, no account. Just open SafeInk and write what the world needs to know.

We scan for identifiers

Our AI checks your letter for names, dates, and details that could identify you — and suggests redactions. All happens in your browser.

Your browser encrypts

Your letter is encrypted with the recipient's public key before it ever leaves your device. We can't read it. Nobody can — except the journalist you chose.

Truth reaches the right people

Your encrypted letter is published to IPFS (uncensorable) and routed to verified journalists, community leaders, or published for everyone.

What we never collect

  • Your IP address (stripped at the edge)
  • Your browser fingerprint (no tracking scripts)
  • Your location (no geolocation)
  • Your name or email (no accounts for writers)
  • Cookies or session tokens (stateless)

What makes SafeInk different

  • Decentralized — no server to shut down or subpoena
  • E2E encrypted — your browser encrypts before sending
  • IPFS storage — uncensorable, permanent
  • Open protocol — anyone can run a node
  • Tor compatible — works over Tor for maximum anonymity
